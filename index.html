About Me

I have spent my career working in marketing for collegiate athletics, sports companies and small colleges. I have had the opportunity to build traditional and digital campaigns that run around the world. I have worked with internal and external design and development teams. Over the past few years of my career I have increasingly interfaced with web developers to build site redesigns and maintain sites.


Working with web developers has opened my eyes to a new world with sites and apps. As I tranistion into a new stage of my career, I look forward to combining my marketing experience with a new set of web development tools. I intend to be a full stack developer and may someday work in a specific field or as consultant.
